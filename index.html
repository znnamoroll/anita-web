About Anita Magsaysay-Ho

Anita Magsaysay-Ho was a renowned Filipino painter known for her portrayals of women in everyday life.

"Women with Baskets" is one of her cherished works that highlights the strength and grace of women in rural settings.

Her distinctive style combines cultural elements with modernist techniques, creating a unique artistic voice that celebrates Filipino identity and womanhood.
